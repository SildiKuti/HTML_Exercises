Grosh

Albanian Grosh is an traditional Albanian dish that is cheap to make and is also known as white bean soup, Fasule and Jani me Fasule.Garlic is the small, white, round bulb of a plant that is related to the onion plant. Garlic has a very strong smell and taste and is used in cooking. Albanians usually eat Grosh with garlic as it gives a nice taste to it.
